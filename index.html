Insurance Management System

×

In one app, the Insurance Management System (IMS) handles all the processes in the customer's insurance business. It automates the process of policy issuance and invoicing up to accounts receivable. It offers real time monitoring of policy status. It allows maintenance of client profiles (total business, claims, etc.) for further analysis. It provides a facility for real time report generation, notification and alerts.

Account management handles password changes, forgotten passwords, log-in/log-off, user addition, deletion, modification, and setting admin rights.
The Dashboard tab features information about expiring policies, outstanding invoices and unpaid Statements of Account (SOA).
The Clients tab shows all client information. It allows addition of clients and modification of details. Per client, you can find, add and edit their corresponding policies, claims and client-specific offers.
The Policies tab shows all policy information. It allows addition, modification, renewal and cancellation of policies. The policy's corresponding endorsements, invoices, claims and attachment can be managed. New options for insurance providers, policy types, clients and agents can be added for new policies.
The Default Rebates tab sets up the default rebates per provider and policy type.
The Invoices tab shows all invoice information. It allows invoice rejection, approval, addition and modification. It handles automatic computations of dues based on premiums, rebates, particulars and taxes. It monitors invoices until marked as paid and associates them with the corresponding official receipts.
The SOA tab keeps track of statement of account statuses, allows addition/modification of SOAs and handles the SOA document creation.
The Claims tab is for addition, editing and monitoring of claims received.
In the Reports tab, reports can be created and downloaded per year, per insurance provider and per policy type. The monthly production of the business can also be shown and downloaded.
In the Forms tab, the company forms can be kept and the available document types can be managed.
In the Company Directory, contact information are added and managed.
PDF generation and sending of email can be done for renewal notices of expiring policies, invoices, statement of accounts. The tabs also feature searching, sorting, filtering and worksheet file download options.

Restaurants and Menus

×

The application keeps a database of restaurants and menus for each restaurant. Users may view, create, edit and delete entries. JSON API endpoints are provided for other apps who might want to gather data from the website. The login and authentication feature restricts edits to protect the database entries.

The search feature allows users to search restaurants or menu items based on entered keywords. Item Catalog: Pokemon Types

The RESTful web application uses the Python framework Flask, implements third-party OAuth authentication, makes use of HTTP methods to perform CRUD (create, read, update and delete) operations and interacts with the database.
